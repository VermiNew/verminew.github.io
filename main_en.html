Snake Project with Neural Networks

What is it?

In the case of the NeuralSnake game using neural networks, the program is able to learn by repeating and analyzing its moves.

At any moment in the game, the network receives information about the position of the point and each part of the snake on the board, and then based on this information, it makes a decision about the action taken, which is executed 512 times per second. This allows the network to learn from its mistakes and gradually become better at the game.

This is a very interesting project because it allows the combination of gaming concepts with artificial intelligence, deep learning, and machine learning. It is also a great way to understand how neural networks work and how they can be used in practical applications.

8-bit music is retro classic for me

What is it & why am I a chiptune creator?

As a enthusiast of creating 8-bit or 16-bit music.
This music is a type of electronic music that originated during the personal computer era and is inspired by music from computer games.
In my creations, I strive to achieve authentic sound using sound synthesizers and special tools like trackers to create compositions that are full of melodic lines and catchy rhythms.
My music is often inspired by classic games and their musical themes, as well as contemporary genres such as vaporwave and synthwave.
Creating 8-bit or 16-bit music is a form of escape for me and a way to express my emotions and ideas.
It also allows me to practice my programming and sound production skills, which is incredibly exciting for me.

I am proud of my projects and hope they will inspire others who also want to create 8-bit or 16-bit music.
