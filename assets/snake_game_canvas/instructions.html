Welcome to Snake Game!

Use arrow keys (or WASD) to control the snake.

Press the space bar to generate a power-up.

Avoid the gray enemies and their bullets.

Eat red food to grow and increase your score.

Collect yellow power-ups for temporary invincibility.

Blue bar represents the time left. If it runs out, the game ends.

Red bar represents your health. Avoid collisions to stay alive!

Press Esc to pause the game.

Press left mouse button to start the game. Unfortunately, the browser safety control is the problem.
